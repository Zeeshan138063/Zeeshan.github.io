Covid-19 Fighter

The motivation behind the development of the COVID-19 Fighter is to facilitate behavioral modification and spreading awareness, as changes to habitual behaviors can minimize the spread of COVID-19. In the fun element, users have to scan their surroundings and hunt funny viruses available weapons in the First Person Shooter style. The player can also switch weapons.

Ohm's Law

Unity3d


One of the most important and basic laws of electrical circuits is Ohm's law which states that the current passing through a conductor is proportional to the voltage over the resistance. See how the equation form of Ohm's law relates to a simple circuit. Adjust the voltage and resistance, and see the current change according to Ohm's law. In this interactive object, learners vary the value of the resistance to see how it affects the current.
